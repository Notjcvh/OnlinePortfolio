Here's the story...

Odyseed is a third-person action adventure game in which you take control of the young red grape known as Merlot who travels the world of Fructerra in his quest to bring safety to his home and to the neighboring nation the Potato Hills. What Merlot discovers is that the world itself has been thrown out of balance by the cruel ruler of the Carrot Khanate, Carrot Khan.

As Merlot progresses in his quest, he will discover the Ancient Seeds, relics that gave birth to every element on their planet, including life itself. These seeds have been taken from the Tree of life and are now guarded by powerful beings who have become corrupted by their power.

Merlot will explore a vast island with the aid of Kiko the Navigator in search of the Ancient Seeds. He will encounter many new cultures and learn of their struggles against the rotting corruption of the Ancient Seeds and each other, working to resolve their struggles in turn.

My Contributions

  • Enemy AI Development

    For this project I designed and implemented pathfinding, behavior trees, and attack mechanisms for multiple enemy types, including Basic Rotten Grape Enemies, Exploding Rotten Grape Enemies, The Grape Boss, Carrot Khan, Carrot Soldier Enemies, and Carrot Lieutenants.

    I also programmed distinct enemy phases based on HP, enhancing the game's challenge and variety.

  • Player Interaction and Abilites

    Developed an AutoLock system for player targeting, optimizing engagement through distance algorithms.

    Programmed seeds to unlock and retain player abilities, ensuring a seamless progression experience.

    Integrated skills from seeds into the UI for a cohesive gameplay flow.

  • Environment and Animation Integration

    Integrated enemies and bosses into the game environment, aligning their animations with attacks for a polished visual experience.

    Scripted UI elements for NPCs, Ability wheel, and HealthBar, contributing to a user-friendly interface.
